Who I Am

About Me

I didn't start in product management — I started on the freight brokerage floor, making cold calls, booking loads, and learning how the supply chain actually moves. That hands-on experience gave me something most PMs don't have: a deep understanding of the people who use the tools I build.

I worked my way from carrier sales into product, where I found my sweet spot — translating complex operational problems into clean, intuitive software. I've shipped billing platforms, fraud prevention workflows, Chrome extensions, mobile apps, and A/B-tested recommendation engines — all in freight and logistics.

Outside of work, I'm teaching myself to build software from scratch. Not because I want to become an engineer, but because I believe the best product people understand what it takes to ship. I'm currently building my first app — a personal project born out of a real problem I wanted to solve.

Side Projects

What I'm Building

I had a real problem, so I went out and built the solution.

Showstr
Your concert history, in the palm of your hand.

I'm an avid concert and festival goer, and for years I tried tracking my shows on spreadsheets, notes apps, and scattered photo albums. None of it felt right — I wanted a single place to catalog every show, relive the memories, and actually see the story of my live music life.

So I built it myself. Showstr is a mobile app that lets you log concerts and festivals, attach photos and videos, search for setlists, add personal notes, and track stats about your concert history — all in one beautiful, private experience.

In Development — Testing Soon
Building Showstr taught me more about app development, coding with AI tools, and the full product lifecycle than any job ever could. It's proof that the best products start with a problem you feel personally.
Log Every Show Catalog concerts and festivals with dates, venues, artists, and personal notes.
Attach Memories Save photos, videos, and setlists to every show. Relive the moments.
Track Your Stats Total shows attended, venues visited, artists seen — your live music life, quantified.
100% Private No login. No cloud. No database. Everything lives on your phone — your memories, your way.
